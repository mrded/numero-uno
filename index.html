What to bring

Passport. You do not want to leave your passport at home and find out about it in the airport.

Flip flops and swimming wear. There is a pool and a sauna in the hotel. Also, we may visit SPA in Merano.

Medical and Travel insurances. Make sure it covers winter sports. Hopefully no one will need to use it.

Euro in cash. Hotel payment can be done only in CASH.

Skiing/Snowboarding equipment. Jacket, trousers, gloves, hat, neck warmer and thermal underwear. Ski/snowboard set, helmet and mask can be rented from the rental shop (details below)

Warm closes for Monday ski show and sledging. We will spend at least an hour outside in the evening.

Good mood 😎

Ski / Snowboard rental

You can rent equipment from Ivan who owns a rental shop in the same village where we staying.

Rental cost was around EUR 100-150 for the entire week. Cards accepted.

You can also swap ski to snowboard and back during the week if you would like to.
